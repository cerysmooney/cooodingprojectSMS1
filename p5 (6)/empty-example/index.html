Honeycomb Sketch

This is my animated sketch. Going along with my bee theme, I decided to create "honeycombs" which are really just hexagons going down the screen with flashing different shades of yellow. The hexagons are also spinning at random times and places. This sketch was to create the scenario of honey dripping down the screen, and let the viewer seem like they are in a beehive.I also added a yellow gradient background, going from light to dark, to add more of an effect.
